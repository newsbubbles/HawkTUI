### Bug Found and Fixed

**Issue**: CLI argument conflict - both `--provider` and `--print` used `-p` short flag.

**Location**: `src/main.rs` lines 46 and 52

**Fix**: Removed `-p` short flag from `--print`, keeping it only for `--provider`.

```diff
-    #[arg(short = 'p', long)]
-    print: bool,
+    #[arg(long)]
+    print: bool,
```

**Status**: ✅ Fixed and rebuilt
